agriculture drones

Innovativeness and efficiency are the basic pillars of modern agriculture.

We at Rubeva know this well, and that is why our company brings you a new product in its portfolio, namely services provided through drone applications.

Advantages of agrodrones

Precise plant monitoring and mapping

The advantage of this technology is that, in addition to precise plant monitoring and mapping, the drone can also spray cultivated crops more gently. One work team with two drones can treat up to 200 hectares of land per day under favorable conditions.

Save time, money and fuel

Using this service saves you time, money and fuel. Plots with obstacles such as electric poles or trees are no problem for the drone. It is equipped with special sensors that are able to automatically stop in the presence of a living object.

Less chemicals - take care of the environment

By using agricultural drones, we can ensure more efficient and uniform spraying, which also saves water and the spray mixture. This is currently very welcome from an ecological point of view.
